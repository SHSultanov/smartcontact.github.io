    • Which smartphones are compatible with card? Do you need an app? Almost all Android smartphones older than 2014 are compatible with the card scanning function. Android devices do not require any special applications. As a rule, the Android nfc reader is located in the center of the back cover of the phone. Before use, you need to enable the nfc function. Scanning a QR code through the camera is also supported. iOS – iPhone XS, XS Max, XR, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max, 12, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max are card compatible and no special apps required. For these models, the nfc reader is located to the right of the rear camera. The QR code can be read through the smartphone's camera. Earlier models of iPhone X, iPhone 8 (plus), iPhone 7 (plus), iPhone SE and iPhone 6S (plus) with IOS 14 installed can read the card signal through the built-in NFC reader, which must be added to the control center. Also, for these iPhone models (except SE and 6S), if 14 IOS is not installed, you can download a special application. A link to additional software is sent upon request. And iPhone 6, 5, 4, etc. support scanning a QR code, which also leads to an electronic business card, through a smartphone camera or special applications for scanning a QR code.
